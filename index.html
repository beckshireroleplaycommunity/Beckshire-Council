Contact us

If you have a question, need help with one of our services, or want to share feedback, our support team is here to help. You can get in touch by messaging the Leader of the Council (@Littlefoxyalex) or by visiting our Discord server (https://discord.gg/rpJRUBU8mA). For general enquiries, residents are encouraged to reach out through the official communication channels provided on the website. Beckshire Council aims to respond to all messages promptly and ensure every enquiry is directed to the right department.

About Beckshire Council

Beckshire Council is the local authority responsible for the Borough of Beckshire, dedicated to delivering high-quality services, supporting local communities, and ensuring a safe, sustainable and thriving place for everyone. Our mission is to make Beckshire a great place to live, work, visit and do business. We provide a wide range of essential services, including waste collection, housing, planning, environmental protection, transport, leisure and community development. Guided by transparency, accountability and public engagement, we work closely with residents, community groups and local businesses to shape a better future for the borough. Through strong partnerships and a shared commitment to fairness and sustainability, Beckshire Council strives to protect the environment, support economic growth, improve public spaces and enhance the quality of life for all who call Beckshire home.
