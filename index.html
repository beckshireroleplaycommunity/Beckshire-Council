About 329 Squadron

Formed to provide aviation-focused activities and training for local youth, 329 Squadron has a long history of community engagement and preparing young people for future success. We welcome cadets aged 12–20 and offer a structured programme of ranks, skills training and adventurous activities.

Eligibility

Aged 12 to 20 at the time of joining. Open to young people of all backgrounds — we promote equality and inclusion.

Uniform & kit

Initial uniform is provided on a loan basis. Cadets are responsible for personal kit for adventurous training — guidance available from staff.
